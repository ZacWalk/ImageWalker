Rotate

Rotate will Rotate or Mirror an image.

Rotate No-Loss

Rotate No-Loss is designed to process JPEG images without loosing quality. Normally every time you re-save a JPEG image quality is lost due to the nature of the JPEG format.

Note: In some cases Rotate No-Loss may remove pixels from the image boundary due to limitations in the way JPEG images are stored. Typically the rotate will be rounded off to the closest 8 pixel boundary.

Tip: Rotating multiple images will be quicker if you use the specialized JPEG Wizard. This wizard is optimized to perform multiple image transformations.
