ImageWalker Support

When reporting a bug please supply the following details:

  • Your email address
  • The version of the software you are running (This can be found on the about box; click on About ImageWalker under the help menu )
  • The operating system you are running (Windows 95/98/ME/NT/2000/XP)
  • The version (if any) of Internet Explorer installed on you computer

NOTE: The ImageWalker program can automatically provide the above information. To see this information click Bug Report under the help menu.

For General Support Questions:

  • Many support questions are already answered in the FAQ. Please read it.
  • Email Support@ImageWalker.com with the details of your query or problem.
  • More information and a list of known problems can be found at http://www.imagewalker.com/support.html
