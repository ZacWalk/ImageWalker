Red Eye Reduction

Red eye reduction reduces the red component of the selected part of an image.

Typically best results can be achieved by zooming in on an image, selecting the red-eye section of an image before applying this filter.

Note: ImageWalker employs a red eye reduction algorithm designed to work on the greatest cross section of images, if it does not work for a specific image it is recommended you use a more advanced application such as Adobe Photoshop.
